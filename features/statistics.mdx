The Statistics view (tap **Reportes** in the bottom navigation) gives you a summary of completed sales for a selected time period. All numbers are computed on the fly from the sales records stored in localStorage — there is nothing to sync or refresh manually.

<Info>
  Statistics only reflect completed sales. Outstanding credit balances are tracked separately in the [Clients](/features/clients) view and are not included in these totals.
</Info>

## Period filters

Three filter buttons at the top of the view control which sales are included:

| Button | Range |
|---|---|
| **Esta Semana** | Current week, starting Sunday at midnight |
| **Este Mes** | Current calendar month from the 1st |
| **Todos** | All sales ever recorded |

Tap a button to switch periods. The summary cards and top products table update immediately.

## Summary cards

Four cards show key totals for the selected period:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Total Ventas" icon="hash">
    Number of completed sales transactions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Ingresos" icon="circle-dollar-sign">
    Total revenue across all sales — cash and credit combined.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Efectivo" icon="banknote">
    Revenue from cash sales only (`tipo: "efectivo"`).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Fiado" icon="clock">
    Revenue from credit sales (`tipo: "fiado"`). This is the billed amount, not the amount collected.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Top products table

Below the summary cards, a ranked table lists up to 10 products sorted by total units sold within the selected period. Each row shows:

- **#** — rank (1 = most sold)
- **Producto** — product name
- **Cantidad** — total units sold
- **Ingresos** — total revenue generated by that product

Products are aggregated by name across all sales in the period. If a product name changed after earlier sales, those earlier sales are counted separately under the old name.
